Vaani

Literacy remains a significant barrier to digital access for over 300 million Indians. This project presents VAANI, an innovative voice-first digital assistant designed to democratize access to digital services regardless of literacy level. The platform enables users to interact entirely through spoken Hindi and Indian languages, eliminating the literacy barrier that prevents access to government schemes, agricultural information, healthcare, financial services, and emergency assistance.
